About the Platform

What is IMAGO Companion?

IMAGO Companion is a supportive platform designed to help you engage more fully in the IMAGO process.

These tools help you organize your thoughts, put words to your experiences, and feel more prepared as you enter dialogue with your partner.

Rather than replacing the workshop or your facilitators, IMAGO Companion supports the process by helping you move from reflection to expression — one step at a time.
